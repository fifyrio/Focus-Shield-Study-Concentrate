Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Focus Shield is designed with privacy first. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • App usage data for blocking functionality
  • Flashcard content you create
  • Study session statistics
  • Device information for app compatibility
How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide app blocking functionality
  • Save your flashcards and progress
  • Generate study statistics
  • Improve app performance

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Local storage on your device
  • Encrypted cloud backup (Pro users)
  • Industry-standard security measures
  • Regular security audits
Third-Party Services

Focus Shield may integrate with:

  • Apple App Store for purchases
  • Analytics services for app improvement
  • Cloud storage for data backup

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your flashcard data
  • Opt-out of analytics

Contact us through the Help Center to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Focus Shield is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Help Center or email us at privacy@focusshield.app
