How do I create a new flashcard deck? ‚ñº
Go to the Decks tab and tap the "+" button. Enter your deck name and start adding flashcards. You can add questions and answers to help you study.
How does app blocking work? ‚ñº
Focus Shield blocks distracting apps when you're in focus mode. To unlock them, you need to complete flashcard questions correctly. This helps you stay focused and learn at the same time.
Can I customize which apps are blocked? ‚ñº
Yes! Go to the Shield tab and select which apps you want to block. You can add or remove apps from your blocked list at any time.
What happens if I answer incorrectly? ‚ñº
If you answer incorrectly, the app will show you the correct answer and you'll need to continue with more questions. This helps reinforce your learning.
How do I upgrade to Pro? ‚ñº
Go to your Profile tab and tap "Upgrade to Pro". You'll get unlimited decks, advanced analytics, and more features to enhance your focus journey.
